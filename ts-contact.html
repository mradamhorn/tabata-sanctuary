Get in Touch!


The Tabata Sanctuary is always looking for help around the place. From a little clean-up to caring for the animals, volunteers are always welcome! We are constantly working with a limited budget but the adorable creatures who call Santuario de Tabata home need constant love and companionship. Help us feed the animals and ensure they have a clean environment for proper rehabilitation.
